23 * QUIC Channel Structure
24 * ======================
25 *
26 * QUIC channel internals. It is intended that only the QUIC_CHANNEL
27 * implementation and the RX depacketiser be allowed to access this structure
28 * directly. As the RX depacketiser has no state of its own and computes over a
29 * QUIC_CHANNEL structure, it can be viewed as an extension of the QUIC_CHANNEL
30 * implementation. While the RX depacketiser could be provided with adequate
31 * accessors to do what it needs, this would weaken the abstraction provided by
32 * the QUIC_CHANNEL to other components; moreover the coupling of the RX
33 * depacketiser to QUIC_CHANNEL internals is too deep and bespoke to make this
